Child Safety & CSAE Standards

Last updated: April 2026

This page describes the publicly posted standards for Groups Link List regarding child safety and our stance against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). It is intended to meet transparency expectations for app distribution platforms.

1. Our commitment

We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, or any content or conduct that sexualizes, endangers, or exploits minors. We prohibit users from posting, sharing, or soliciting such material through features of this app.

2. What is not allowed

  • CSAM or any visual depiction of child sexual abuse (illegal everywhere we operate).
  • Sexualization of minors, grooming behaviour, or predatory contact toward minors.
  • Links or instructions intended to obtain illegal or harmful content involving minors.

3. Reporting

If you become aware of content or behaviour in Groups Link List that raises child safety concerns, please use the in-app reporting options where available, and/or contact us at the email below. We take reports seriously and review them as promptly as practicable.

4. Law enforcement & authorities

Where required by applicable law, we will preserve relevant information and cooperate with competent authorities in good faith, subject to valid legal process and our technical capabilities.

5. Changes

We may update this page to reflect changes in laws, platform requirements, or our practices. The “Last updated” date will be revised when material changes are made.
