Privacy Policy

Read More Message Generator App

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the Read More Message Generator app handles data, what we collect (if any), and how we use it. The app is designed to work locally on your device as much as possible.

2. Data We Collect

The app does not send any personal data to our servers because it does not use our own server. What actually happens:

  • Local data only: The text you enter (visible text, hidden text, templates) is stored on your device only using local storage (e.g. Shared Preferences). We do not review it or send it to any third party.
  • No sign-in: We do not ask for your email, phone number, or any account.
  • Sharing to WhatsApp: When you use "Share to WhatsApp", the WhatsApp app on your device opens with the prepared text only. We do not receive or store the content of your messages.

3. Ads and Third Parties

The app displays ads from Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers, IP address, and app usage data for personalized advertising and analytics. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob and Privacy

We do not control the data that Google collects through ads.

4. Storage and Security

All templates and settings (such as dark mode) are stored on your device only. We do not transfer them to the internet. Uninstalling the app removes this data from your device.

5. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data.
  • Manage ad settings through your device settings or Google settings.
  • Choose not to use the "Share to WhatsApp" feature if you do not want to open WhatsApp from the app.

6. Children

The app is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can address it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated version. We recommend visiting this page occasionally.

8. Contact Us

For any questions about privacy or this policy, please contact us via the email or official channel listed in the app store (Google Play / App Store).
